Meet the Doctor

Dr. Alexander Mazurovsky was born and raised in Odessa, Ukraine. He immigrated to America when he was 22 in pursuit of higher education. In college, Dr. Mazurovsky developed a passion for patient care and natural treatments. In 1998 he graduated with a BS from excelsior college, and in 1999 graduated from New York Chiropractic College with a DC degree. Dr. Alexander Mazurovsky has been practicing for 20 years with offices in Staten Island and Brooklyn, New York. In 2020, he became an RN certified by the International College of Health Sciences.
